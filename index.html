Let's stop Corruption in draught area's

India is the country where the drought problem in villages is a major issue. Every year people have to suffer from the problem of water scarcity, although government allocate water tanker to each village but it is not guaranteed that it will reach to that particular village on time. So we focus on the water supply in drought areas in India. Our project is based on water tanker reach ability, monitoring and controlling system in which the numbers of tankers provided to the particular village are checked by detecting the signals from tankers to the well over some specific distance. It also checks the water level in well by using sensors. Each village is having a server which is able to keep records of all the water tankers with date and time and water level in well. These records are accessed by an administrator in the home ministry which is connected to each other through network.
